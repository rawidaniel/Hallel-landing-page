Our Achievements

Hallel is a major player in Ethiopia's import and export sector and has been for over a decade. Our focus on quality and customer satisfaction has earned us recognition from the Ethiopian government as a Platinum taxpayer. We collaborate with automobile manufacturing companies in Europe, Dubai, and other locations to provide a wide range of automobiles to our customers. This ensures that we offer the latest models and technologies.

Our past collaborations with large Ethiopian firms have allowed us to meet the growing demand for automobiles in the country. We are committed to maintaining our industry leadership by consistently providing high- quality services to our clients.
