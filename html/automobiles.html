ABOUT OUR AUTOMOBILES

At Hallel Import Export, we specialize in importing and selling high- quality automobiles to our clients in Ethiopia. Over the years, we have continually evolved our business to meet the changing needs of our clients. In the beginning, we started with used automobiles, including popular models such as Vitz, Yaris, Platz, Corolla, and Belta.

However, we recognized the growing demand for new automobiles in Ethiopia, and we made the strategic decision to shift our focus to importing and selling brand new automobiles with duty-free incentives. We are now proud to offer our clients access to some of the most iconic brands in the industry, including double cabin pickups, hind cabins, Toyota Rush, Hyundai Kereta, Tusang, Chevrolet Captiva, and more.

All of our automobiles undergo rigorous inspections and testing to ensure that they meet our high standards of quality and reliability. With our duty-free incentives, we are also able to offer our clients competitive pricing, making it more affordable than ever to own a brand new automobile in Ethiopia.
