ABOUT OUR ELECTRICAL VEHICLES

We take pride in providing our customers with a diverse range of cars to choose from, including both electric and gas-powered vehicles.

We support the Ethiopian government's policies towards EV's and understand that our customers have different preferences and needs when it comes to cars, and that's why we strive to offer something for everyone.

Our electric car lineup is one of the most extensive in the market, featuring popular models such as the Volkswagen ID4 and ID6, Mercedes EQ6, Peugeot 208, Citroen EC4, Honda Enpy, Toyota Bizzy 4X, Honda EC, and Beng Beng Easter.
