Warranty

We offer a guarantee or warranty on our automobiles based on manufacturing defects. This guarantee is valid for up to six months or 20,000-25,000 km, whichever comes first. We stand behind the quality and reliability of our automobiles and want our clients to have peace of mind when purchasing an automobile from us

Payment installment or loan services

We provide payment installment and loan services, allowing clients to make a 50% prepayment and finance the rest. Partnering with Ethiopian banks like Commercial Bank of Ethiopia, Awash Bank, and others, we aim to offer flexible, affordable payment solutions tailored to individual needs.

Customer Satisfaction

Customer satisfaction is vital to our car importing business. Recognizing the importance of car purchases, we prioritize quality and performance in every imported vehicle. Our dedication extends beyond the sale, emphasizing lasting relationships with post-purchase support. Each content customer bolsters our reputation and drives us to constantly improve our services.

Our Mission

At Hallel, we believe in the power of community and the importance of taking action. Our mission is to create a better world by living the values of WE WORK, WE SERVE, WE EARN

1. WE WORK

WE WORK tirelessly to provide our clients with the best possible service and solutions. We are committed to delivering excellence in everything we do, from the smallest task to the biggest project

2. WE SERVE

WE SERVE by making a positive impact on our community and the world around us. We believe that serving others is our ultimate purpose and strive to make a difference in the lives of those we encounter

3. WE EARN

WE EARN by creating value for our clients and ourselves. We believe that success is a result of hard work, dedication, and a commitment to our values. We are proud to earn our clients' trust and loyalty through our exceptional service and solutions

Our Vision

At Hallel Import Export, we are revolutionizing the automotive industry with our state- of-the-art assembly factory, setting the standard for excellence and innovation. But our vision doesn't stop there. We are breaking new ground in the real estate market, providing affordable and sustainable housing options that exceed expectations. And we are making an impact on a global scale, exporting agricultural commodities and contributing to the growth and development of Ethiopia's Economy.
